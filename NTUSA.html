Being a Representative of NTU

Since I was a freshman, I have been elected as one of the National Taiwan University Student Ambassadors. As a student ambassador, I helped our school organize various international meetings and ceremonies. I also had the opportunities to greet many esteemed guests and take them on school tours. After every mission I always feel really satisfied and blessed because of the lessons I learned, including being cautious and prudent during preparation, maintaining etiquette while interacting with guests, being reliable and composed when something unplanned happens, and also arranging the data clearly and efficiently.

How to promote our school appropriately and accurately to the esteemed guests is a skill we must acquire, and for every mission to be accomplished perfectly, we student ambassadors have to work in close cooperation, because every one of us represents NTU, it would be embarrassing if we make mistakes.




The most interesting experience I gained is how to interact with people from different countries. Through the various school tours I have given , I learned how to grasp attention from people from different age group and occupations.
